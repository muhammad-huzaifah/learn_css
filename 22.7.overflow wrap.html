The overflow-wrap Property

overflow-wrap: normal (default):

This div contains a very long word: thisisaververveverververververlongword. The long word will not break and wrap to the next line

Overflow-wrap: break-word

This div contains a very long word: thisisaververveverververververlongword. The long word will break and wrap to the next line.

Overflow-wrap: anywhere:

This div contains a ver long word: thisisaververververververververlongword. The long word will break and wrap to he next line.
